(Das ist ja, was R[icardo] sagt. Die proportion of both cannot increase, and if the portion of both increases, it cannot increase in the same proportion, as otherwise portion and proportion would be identical. The proportion of the one cannot increase but by that of the other decreasing.1 Daß Herr B[ailey] nun die portion of the labour „value" of „wages"2 nennt und die proportion value of „profits", that the same commodity has 2 values for him, one in the hand of the labourer, and the other in the hand of the capitalist, is a nonsense of his own3.)
