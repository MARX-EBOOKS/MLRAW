But how to express A in B or C? A, B, C must all be considered as something different from what they are as things, products, values in use, to express „them“ in each other, or, what comes to the same thing, to treat them as equivalent expressions of the same unity. A = 4 B. Then the value of A is expressed in 4 B, and the value of 4 B in A, so that both sides express the same. They are equivalents. They are both equal expressions of value. It would be the same if they were unequal ones or A > 4 B, A < 4 B. In all these cases they are as far ||335|| as they are values, only different or equal in quantity, but always quantities of the same quality. The difficulty is to find this quality.1
