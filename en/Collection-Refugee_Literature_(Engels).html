Refugee Literature (Engels)

From Marxists-en
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Keywords:Refugee Literature (Engels)
SMW 🔍
Date of beginning May 1874
Date of end April 1875


Keywords: Emigration, Refugees
Written from mid-May 1874 to April 1875. The articles were published as they were completed in the German Social-Democrats' newspaper, Der Volksstaat.
In 1875, Article V appeared as a separate pamphlet under the title Soziales aus Rußland.
Articles I, II and V were included by Engels in a collection of his works, Internationales aus dem "Volksstaat" (1871-75), Berlin, 1894. Individual articles were published in English, in full or abridged, in a number of collections.
Published in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45
  • I. A Polish Proclamation
  • II. Programme of the Blanquist Commune Refugees

  • V. On Social Relations in Russia
