Conversation (falsified) with a New York Herald correspondent, July 20, 1871

From Marxists-en
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 20 July 1871


Collection(s): Le Gaulois, New York Herald
Keywords : Defamation, Interview, Paris Commune
This text is not available, or incomplete, please help if you can !

Marx had a conversation with a New York Herald correspondent on July 20, 1871, and this newspaper printed on August 3 a deliberately falsified report (see also Marx’s letter to Friedrich Bolte of August 25, 1871).

Marx send a protest letter to the New York Herald on August 17, 1871, which was not published.

The french newspaper Le Gaulois printed extracts from the New York Herald article on August 22.
