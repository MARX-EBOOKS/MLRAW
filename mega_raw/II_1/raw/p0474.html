“Treating labour as a commodity, and capital, the produce of labour, as
another, then, if the value of these 2 commodities were regulated by equal
quantities of labour, a given amount of labour would, under all circum-
stances, exchange for that quantity of capital which had been produced by
the same amount of labour; antecedent labour would always exchange for
the same amount as present labour. Aber the value of labour, in relation to
