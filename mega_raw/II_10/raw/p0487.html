32) “The wages of labour depend upon the price of labour and the quantity of labour per-
formed ... An increase in the wages of labour does not necessarily imply an enhancement of
the price of labour. From fuller employment, and greater exertions, the wages of labour may
be considerably increased, while the price of labour may continue the same.” (West l. c. p. 67,
