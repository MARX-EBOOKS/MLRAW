1060. The investment in railways appears to have affected the value of

tion of the trading community, who may have been forced to abstract from

do not believe that it has been the cause of any material mischief to the pre-
sent time. It has principally raised the value of money invested in railway
debentures and stocks, which has tended to increase the difficulties of the
directors in raising the money which they require to carry on their works.
