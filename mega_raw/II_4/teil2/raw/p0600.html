4868. (Chapman) Quantity of money. “If the money coming from the divi-
dends into the hands of the public is not counteracted by repayment of
loans to the Bank, large sums become unoccupied; and it is better worth
the while of the persons receiving those dividends to take a lower rate of
discount than to keep their money unemployed, and therefore we get very
much below the Bank then.”
